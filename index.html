// 01 — Who I am

A rare blend of bench science and builder instinct.

I'm a microbiologist and fermentation scientist by training — yeast genetics, bioinformatics, and brewing & distilling science. I spent seven years as Yeast Specialist at New Belgium Brewing, co-founded a craft distillery grain-to-glass, chaired the Microbiology committee of the ASBC, and taught for White Labs' professional program.

Along the way I became equally fluent in code. Today I design and build agentic AI tools, software, and websites — bringing the same Six Sigma rigor I used to stabilize fermentations to the way I ship products. The result: a single person who can walk a production floor, debug a contamination, and then hand you a dashboard that tracks it.

I work with Asheville breweries, makers, restaurants, farms, and nonprofits — and with clients nationally who need science-grade thinking and modern tooling in one place.
